Assignment

Prime Number Game

A simple SwiftUI game where the player determines whether a displayed number is prime or not before the timer runs out. The game tracks correct and incorrect answers and ends after 10 rounds.

The game generates a random number between 1 and 100, and the player must choose "Prime" or "Not Prime" before a 5-second timer expires. If time runs out, the answer is marked incorrect. The game tracks correct and incorrect answers, displaying a score after 10 rounds. Visual feedback (✔️ or ❌) is given for each selection, and a new round begins after every answer. At the end of 10 rounds, a Game Over screen appears.
